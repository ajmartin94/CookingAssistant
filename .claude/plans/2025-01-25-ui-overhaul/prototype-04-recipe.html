Honey Garlic Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

35 min total 4 servings Medium difficulty 520 cal/serving

Ingredients

4
  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • to taste salt and pepper

Instructions

1

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

2

In a small bowl, whisk together honey, minced garlic, soy sauce, and olive oil to make the glaze.

3

Arrange broccoli florets and cherry tomatoes on one side of the baking sheet. Drizzle with a little olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

4

Place salmon fillets on the other side of the baking sheet. Brush generously with the honey garlic glaze, reserving some for later.

5

Bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon is cooked through and vegetables are tender. Brush salmon with remaining glaze halfway through.

6

Remove from oven and let rest for 2 minutes. Serve salmon over the roasted vegetables and enjoy!
