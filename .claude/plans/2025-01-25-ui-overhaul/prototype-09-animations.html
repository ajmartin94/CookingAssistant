Animation Philosophy
Animations should be purposeful (communicates state), quick (200-400ms for most), and consistent (same easing curves throughout). Use cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) for most interactions — it's the "ease-out" feel that makes things feel responsive. Reserve bounce/overshoot for celebrations.
