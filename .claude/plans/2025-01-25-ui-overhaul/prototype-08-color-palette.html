How Seasonal Themes Work
The accent color shifts based on season selection. The base mode (light/dark) stays the same, but buttons, highlights, active states, and subtle tints adapt. Fall (#e07850) is the default accent.

Emphatic Theme Application
Seasonal themes affect: page background gradients, sidebar/header tints, active nav states, card borders & shadows, metadata text, tags/badges, button gradients & glow effects. The season should be felt, not just seen on one button.
